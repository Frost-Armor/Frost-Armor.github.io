Biography

Ruikang Zhong received the B.Eng. degree in electronic information engineering from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC), Chengdu, China, in 2016, and the M.Sc. degree in wireless communication from the University of Southampton, Southampton, U.K., in 2018. He is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree with the Queen Mary University of London, London, U.K.,His research interests include the unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV)-aided communication systems, reinforcement learning, non-orthogonal multiple access (NOMA) techniques, and IRSs/RISs-aided wireless networks.
